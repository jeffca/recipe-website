Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken thighs
  • 1 teaspoon Garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon Smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves Garlic, grated
  • 1 tablespoons Ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons Butter
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 3 Diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup Heavy cream
  • Cilantro, chopped
  • Salt, to taste
  • Cooked white rice

Tools

  • Medium bowl
  • Knife
  • Frying pan
  • Immersion blender

Instructions

  1. Prepare the chicken.
  2. Cut chicken thigh into bite-size pieces.
  3. Add salt, garam masala, paprika, cayenne pepper, garlic, ginger and greek yogurt.
  4. Mix well.
  5. Marinate it for an hour or overnight.
  6. Slice the onion.
  7. Mince garlic and ginger.
  8. Prepare a pan with butter and add to medium heat.
  9. Add marinated chicken and cook well.
  10. Remove the chicken.
  11. Add the onion, ginger and garlic to the pan. Cook for a few minutes.
  12. Add paprika and tomatoes.
  13. Let it cook for several minutes.
  14. Add cream.
  15. Use an immersion blender to better incorporate ingredients.
  16. Add back chicken. Stir well.
  17. Add more cream as necessary and cilantro.
  18. Serve over rice.
