Caesar Salad

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 teaspoons Anchovy paste
  • 1/2 cup Olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese
  • Crutons
  • 1/2 lemon for juice
  • Salt

Tools

  • Small bowl for dressing
  • Large bowl for salad
  • Spoon
  • Knife
  • Lettuce strainer

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dressing.
  2. Mix dijon mustard, red wine vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, garlic and anchovy paste in a bowl.
  3. Drizzle in olive oil slowly while whisking.
  4. Add parmesan cheese.
  5. Let the dressing rest for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Prepare the romaine lettuce.
  7. Wash lettuce then dry in a strainer.
  8. Cut lettuce into bite size pieces.
  9. Plate salad in a bowl or plate.
  10. Add croutons, parmesan cheese, lemon juice and salt.
  11. Toss well.
  12. Add the dressing and toss again before serving.
