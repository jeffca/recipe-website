Meatball Sandwich

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Bread Flour (or All Purpose)
  • Sugar
  • 15 tomatoes on the vine
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • Rosemary
  • Basil
  • 4 links spicy Italian sausage
  • 16 oz ground beef
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 egg
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Tools

  • Mixing bowls
  • Baking sheets
  • Crock pot
  • Immersion blender
  • Wooden spoon

Instructions

  1. Bake the bread.
  2. Add yeast, warm water and sugar to a mug. Mix well. Give the yeast several minutes to start growing.
  3. Add flour and salt to a mixing bowl.
  4. Combine the yeast-water with the flour. Mix well.
  5. Let the dough rest for 1 hour. Knead the dough well after it rests.
  6. Let the dough rest for another 2 hours.
  7. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees.
  8. Form the dough into a baguette shape and place onto a baking sheet.
  9. Bake in an oven at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
  10. Make the marinara sauce.
  11. Add sliced on the vine tomatoes into a crock pot.
  12. Add sliced onion and garlic into a pan on medium-low heat. Cook lightly.
  13. Add onion and garlic to the crock pot, along with rosemary, basil, salt and pepper.
  14. Cook for 4-8 hours on either high or low heat. Stir the crock pot every 1-2 hours.
  15. Once the tomatoes have been cooked down into a sauce, turn the crock pot off.
  16. Use an immersion blender to completely mix the ingredients.
  17. Use wooden spoon to scoop out the sauce and empty the contents into a strainer.
  18. Capture the tomato juice in a separate receptacle then put the sauce in Tupperware.
  19. Make the meatballs.
  20. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees.
  21. Combine sausage, ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, one stirred egg, rosemary, thyme, salt and pepper into a mixing bowl. Mix well.
  22. Form into meatballs.
  23. Add to a baking sheet.
  24. Bake the meatballs at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.
  25. Combine meatballs, marinara sauce, toasted bread and melted parmesan cheese to complete the sandwich.
