Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 4 large hass avocados
  • 2 tomatoes on the vine
  • 1/2 sweet onion
  • 2 small lemons
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • salt

Tools

  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Chopper
  • Juicer
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Avocado slicer

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients together.
  2. Gather the tools.
  3. Cut avocados in half.
  4. Use the avocado slicer and slice the avocados into thin slices.
  5. Add avocado slices to the mixing bowl.
  6. Using the juicer, squeeze 1 and 1/2 half lemon juice onto the avocados. Mix well alongside salt.
  7. Using the chopper, chop the tomatoes into small chunks.
  8. Add salt to the chopped tomatoes.
  9. Transfer the chopped tomatoes, along with the tomato juice, into the mixing bowl.
  10. Using the chopper, chop the onion into small chunks.
  11. Transfer the onion chunks into the mixing bowl.
  12. Using the juicer, squeeze the remaining 1/2 lemon juice into the mixing bowl.
  13. Mix all ingredients together.
  14. Cut cilantro into small pieces.
  15. Mix the cilantro into the mixing bowl. Stir well.
  16. Add salt as necessary.

Tips & Tricks

Buy avocados that have not yet ripened. Check on them every 12 hours. Once they are a little bit soft, transfer them to the fridge.

By chilling the avocados before cooking with them, the guacamole will be chilled and ready to eat once you are done preparing it.

Buy the cilantro only a few days before making the guacamole. Cilantro spoils quickly.
