Shrimp Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Shrimp
  • Shredded cabbage
  • 1 medium Carrot
  • 1 Jalapeno
  • Cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon Honey
  • 2 Limes for juice
  • 1 Avocado
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1/2 cup Kewpie mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Paprika
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Tortillas

Tools

  • Mandolin
  • Knife
  • Medium bowl
  • Strainer
  • Juicer

Instructions

  1. Prepare the coleslaw.
  2. Slice or use a mandolin on cabbage.
  3. Dice carrot, dice a jalapeno with seeds removed, and chop cilantro.
  4. Add all to a mixing bowl.
  5. Add salt, honey and lime juice. Stir well.
  6. Prepare the mayonnaise.
  7. Chop garlic.
  8. Add Kewpie mayonnaise, lime juice and garlic to a mixing bowl.
  9. Stir well.
  10. Start warming up a tortilla in the toaster oven.
  11. Prepare the shrimp.
  12. Peel shell off shrimp.
  13. Cut shrimp into rectangular or square pieces. Add shrimp to a bowl.
  14. Add salt, paprika, chili powder, onion powder and garlic powder to the bowl. Mix well.
  15. Add seasoned shrimp to a hot pan.
  16. Flip the shrimp after 90 seconds of cooking.
  17. Continue to cook the shrimp until it is fully cooked, about 3 minutes.
  18. Assemble the taco.
  19. Use a strainer to remove all the water or juice from the shrimp and coleslaw to preserve a dry tortilla.
  20. Take the tortilla out of the toaster oven and add coleslaw mix, mayonnaise, and shrimp.
  21. Serve with sliced avocado or fresh lime juice.
