Thai Chili Chicken

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl with raw chicken thigh, 1:1 ratio of fish sauce & lemon juice.
  2. Add one stalk of green onion cut into two inch pieces.
  3. Add crushed garlic.
  4. Add one spoon of cornstarch.
  5. Drizzle white pepper and salt.
  6. Mix well.
  7. Cover, refrigerate and marinate for 1-2 hours.
  8. Prep the sauce.
  9. Chop cilantro, green onion, jalape√±o or chili pepper, and minced garlic.
  10. Add 1:1 ratio of fish sauce, lemon juice and water.
  11. Add one spoonful of soy sauce.
  12. Add two spoonfuls of sugar.
  13. Mix well.
  14. Refrigerate.
  15. Once the chicken is finished marinating, add potato starch to a pan.
  16. Cover the chicken evenly with the potato starch.
  17. Now turn the stove on to medium heat.
  18. Add 1 cm on oil to the pan.
  19. Cook the chicken while flipping on both sides.
